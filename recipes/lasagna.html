Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe is a beloved family favorite since 1960. I hope that you can take this recipe and make a delicious meal for you and your family to enjoy for generations.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz package lasagna noodles
  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot lightly salted water to a boil
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Brown beef and season with salt and pepper;drain
  4. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 mins
  5. Combine mozzarella and ricotta; stir well
  6. Alternate layers of noodles, meat and cheese in a 9x13 inch pan
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, until cheese is melted

Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Another Brilliant meal that will leave you refreshed. As you can tell, I love italian food!

Ingredients

  • 2 packages Angel Hair Pasta
  • 3 cups, Tomato sauce
  • 2tbs Salt
  • 1tbs basil
  • 1lb Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in a pan and bring it to a simmer for 6 mins
  2. Put the tomato sauce in a seperate pan to a simmer for 5 mins
  3. Put ground beef in a pan and cook it on high until browned
  4. Mix it together and enjoy!
